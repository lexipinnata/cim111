How to Make a Paper Airplane

Step 1

Fold your piece of paper in half length-wise to make a crease

Step 2

Fold the top corners into the line

Step 3

Fold the sheet length-wise again

Step 4

Fold down both top corners again twice

Step 5

Pull out the wings of the paper to make it look like a plane
